At Height Images

At Height Images (AHI), based in Sacramento, CA, provides aerial and commercial photography services.

The Brief

AHI was looking for a brand identity with a clean aesthetic that would be easy to distinguish. A logo with geometric based forms and inclusion of text was preferred with the following characteristics: airy, clean, simple, minimal, and straightforward.

Brainstorming

Various sketches were made based on text, abstraction, and equipment-based elements to explore silhouettes and forms that would help their logo stand out.

Finalizing

Concept 3 was approved as the logo. A brand guide was developed with along with a business card and internal documents.

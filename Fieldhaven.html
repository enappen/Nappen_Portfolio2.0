FieldHaven Marketplace

FieldHaven Marketplace, located in Lincoln, CA, is a thrift store whose profits support the FieldHaven Feline Center, their affiliated nonprofit animal welfare organization.

The Brief

FieldHaven Marketplace was looking for a social media manager to reach their goal of increasing their online presence and increase sales. As merchandise is frequently updated, a consistent posting schedule was needed. Posting should be made on Facebook, TikTok, and Instagram.

Research

In order to tailor content that would best work for FieldHaven, I developed a social media strategy plan to establish goals based on research that effects their direct market and helps identify their target audience. The layout works with FieldHaven's brand identity in the color scheme and typefaces. The layout is presented in a way to take dense research and break it down in a easy-to-digest format.

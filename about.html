About Me

I'm a multimedia designer currently shifting into full stack web development. I approach every project through human-centered design, prioritizing the target audience and core message.

Most of my background is in marketing campaigns, layout design, motion graphics, and branding. Lately, I've been expanding into development, working with HTML, CSS, and C++, and building projects that prioritize accessibility.

I've spent over five years refining my design skills in Adobe Creative Cloud, supported by an A.S. in Design and Digital Media and certifications in Game Design and Animation & 3D Modeling. I enjoy approaching problems from multiple angles to create solutions that feel intuitive and engaging.

After freelancing as a designer, I discovered a passion for full stack development. The mix of analytical and creative problem-solving sparked my interest. I'm currently finishing my bachelor's degree in Full Stack Web Development and am focused on building accessible applications that people genuinely enjoy using.

When I am not designing, you'll usually find me researching something new or out in the garden.
